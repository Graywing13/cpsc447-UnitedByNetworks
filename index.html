Social Capital represents

the potency of a person's social network and their connection to the community. Numerous individuals have contended that it plays a pivotal role in facilitating upward mobility, underscoring the vital role of social capital and the potential of social interconnectedness in enhancing our communities. To contribute to the understanding of this critical dimension of society, our visualization offers insights into social capital across colleges throughout the United States.
